Add a cache

Every listing is checked by hand before it goes live. Here's how a submission gets from you to the map.

  1. Fill in the form

    Give it a name, the area, the what3words address, and a difficulty. A hint is optional but appreciated.

  2. It gets reviewed

    We check the address resolves to a sensible spot and the listing follows the etiquette below. This usually takes a few days.

  3. It's added to the list

    Once approved, your cache appears on the browse page for everyone to find.

What we ask for

title: "Old Lockgate Lookout"
area: "Runcorn, Cheshire"
what3words: ///bridge.otter.cinema
difficulty: Easy | Medium | Hard
size: Micro | Small | Regular | Large
description: a couple of sentences
hint: optional
your name: optional, shown as "added by"

A few ground rules

Only list caches on land you have permission to place them on, and keep them well clear of roads, rail, and private gardens. Don't bury anything — a hidden container tucked in or under something is enough. Cache in, trash out: leave the spot as good as you found it. Nothing that needs a permit, a ladder, or a lockpick.
